Welcome to the Wavecore demo

Suggested testing procedure:
1. Ensure your browser has permissions to your device's microphone.
2. Click the "LAUNCH DEMO" button.
3. When ready, begin recording by clicking the "REC" button.
4. While recording, leave room at the beginning to demonstrate Wavecore's silence detection abilities.
5. Once "silence" is logged to the console, begin speaking into the microphone. "speech" will get logged to the console.
6. After a while, try talking LOUDLY into the mic or tapping it with your finger. This should cause a mic clip, and the console will log "clipping".
7. When done, click "STOP" to save the audio and stop recording.
8. Click the "SPLIT" button, to see Wavecore split itself into different segments based on the silence, speech, and clipping detection.
9. Clicking on the individual split segment buttons allows you to hear just that portion of the overall recording.
